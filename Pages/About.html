I 'm what's known as a GenXer. This means that I am at home with the latest technology, but I also know how to use a rotary phone. I've been working in the manufacturing industry for almost 30 years, first in IT at a major manufacturer of airplane wings, next managing factory maintenance,

I started when I was 12 and continued well into my adult years. I

I spent alot of time in the gym, lifting weights. When I hit my forties, I changed
course and began triathlons. I've completed over a dozen triathlons, two
dozen running events, mostly half marathons, and one marathon. Of course
it's not all physical, I enjoy bingeing home improvement shows, crime dramas
and science fiction. Oddly, computers were never a hobby. For me, it's always
